How to play?

Kill players and fight whilst you avoid demons and destroyers. Use WASD to move and E to pick up items and open doors. Basic Crafting:

  • 20 of the main ore and 15 iron to make pickaxes and hammers after stone.
  • 20 main ore and 10 iron for axes.
  • 10 main ore, 10 iron, and 10 of the ore below the main iron or stone if the ore below is iron for swords.
